Banned game — Kolejka

“Kolejka”, “queue” or “line” in Polish is a board game launched in February 2011 by Poland's Institute of National Remembrance.It was designed to teach young generations about the hardships under communism (1945–1989), particularly the difficulty of acquiring consumer goods in the shortage economy. Due to senetive topic, it was banned by in Russia.

A fairly interesting family game with political theme. Players act as a family to collect required set of goods for festival or event in the period of shortage. Introduction of too many luck factor makes the game a little bit chaotic.

Essen 2017 — Calimala

“Calimala” refers to the a great guild of the cloth finishers and merchants in foreign cloth, based at Florence.

Typical theme of european games. The main mechanism of the game is “Worker placement”, placing the worker between actions and execute both of them, the earlier comers will receive an action bonus. In total, here are 15 scoring criteria, each may be activated only once or less.

The deluxe laser cut building kit costs only €10 only!!!
